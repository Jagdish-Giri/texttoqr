# Text to QR

A stylish web app to generate QR codes from any text, with cinematic animations and effects.

## Features

- Enter any text and generate a high-quality QR code instantly.
- Cinematic animation: loading spinner, hammer smash, glass crack, and QR reveal.
- Animated honeycomb background and QR pop-in effect.
- Download QR as PNG.
- Fully responsive and works in all modern browsers.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Type or paste your text in the box.
3. Click **Generate QR**.
4. Enjoy the animation and scan or download your QR code.
